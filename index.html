Our Vision

Iraq Green Building Council - IQGBC

The Iraqi Green Building Council initiates green buildings because they are healthier for people and the planet. We believe green buildings are a cost-effective solution for carbon reduction, job creation, and innovation. By transforming our communities with zero-carbon green buildings, we can put Iraq on a path to a more sustainable future.
